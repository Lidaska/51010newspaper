India's First Solar-Powered Bullet Train Unveiled

India has launched its inaugural solar-powered bullet train, connecting Mumbai and Ahmedabad. This eco-friendly initiative aims to revolutionize the country's transportation system by reducing carbon emissions and promoting sustainable energy sources.

The train features state-of-the-art technology, including solar panels installed on its roof, which generate electricity to power onboard systems. This marks a significant milestone in India's commitment to renewable energy and modernizing its railway infrastructure.
